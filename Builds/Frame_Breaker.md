# Frame Breaker
**Frame Breaker** is a full Monk build that focuses on "Hit & Run" tactics while dealing solid amounts of damage. This build provides a variety of active ki-dependent abilities to ensure gameplay is highly versatile.

## Leveling Up ‚¨ÜÔ∏è
This build becomes stronger over time by increasing modifiers and proficiency bonuses while granting more active abilities.

At **3rd level**, choose the [Way of Mercy subclass](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/monk:mercy). It is considered one of the best Monk subclasses and provides the user with the ability to heal and deal extra damage.

The core of this build is **4th level**. At this level, grab the [Mobile feat](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/feat:mobile) to avoid enemy attacks and move freely.

At **5th level**, we gain an Extra Attack, which is a great addition to our build.

Lastly, the further you progress, the faster you become. For example, at **6th level**, your movement speed is:

**`35 (Wood Elf) + 15 (Class) + 10 (Feat) = 60 ft.`**

This is double the movement speed of regular humanoids. When you duel a creature, there is almost no way it can catch and attack you in the same turn. 

---

## Combat Mechanics & Synergy ‚ö°
On your turn at 5th level, use your regular action to attack with your longsword. Extra Attack lets you do this twice. Then, as a bonus action, you can attack with an unarmed strike.

Thanks to the **Mobile** feat, you can then run as far as possible without triggering opportunity attacks from the enemies you attacked.

If every hit lands, the damage for one turn will be:

**`2d10 + 1d6 + 9 (DEX)`** *damage*.

While this amount may not seem massive, you can also use active abilities (like Hand of Harm) to deal more damage or heal an ally.
