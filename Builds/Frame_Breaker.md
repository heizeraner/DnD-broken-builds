# Frame Breaker
**Frame Breaker** is a full Monk build that focuses on "Hit & Run" tactic, while dealing solid amounts of damage. Also, this build is provided with lots of active ki-dependant abilities to ensure that gameplay is very variable.

## Leveling Up ‚¨ÜÔ∏è
This build becomes stronger over time by increasing modifiers, proficiency bonuses and gives more active abilities.

At **3rd level**, choose the [Way of Mercy subclass](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/monk:mercy). It is considered one of the best monk subclasses and provides user with ability to heal and deal damage.

The core of this build is **4th level**. At this level, grab the [Mobile feat](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/feat:mobile) to be able to avoid enemy attacks.

At **5th level**, we gain an Extra Attack whcih is a greate addition to our build.

Lastly, as further you progress, the faster you are. For example on **6th level** you have movement speed:

**`35 (Wood elf) + 15 (class) + 10 (feat) = 60 ft.`**

This is a double movement speed of regular humanoids, so when you duel a creature there is almost no way it can catch and attacks you in the same turn.

## Combat Mechanics & Synergy ‚ö°
On your turn at 5th level, use your regular action to attack with your longsword. Extra Attack lets you do this twice. Then, as a bonus action, you can attack with your unarmed strike.

Then you can run as far as possible without getting any opportunity attacks from enemies.

If every shot hits, the damage for one turn will be:

**`2d10 + 1d6 + 9 (DEX)`** *damage*.

And this amount may not sound very powerful but you can also use a few of the active abilities to deal more or heal someone.
